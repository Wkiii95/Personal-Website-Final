Extracurricular activities

Music

In first grade of primary school, I began to explore classical music and started learning the violin and piano. This brought me numerous performance and competition experiences, enhancing my self-confidence. As I entered middle school, I delved into popular music and taught myself various instruments. I also had the opportunity to form a band and perform with like-minded individuals. These experiences strengthened my self-learning abilities and communication skills.

CDSJ5 Student Union

From April 27th to 30th, 2023, the student Union participated in the "2023 Tap Siac Craft Market," setting up booths at Tashi Square to sell original products designed by the student council. This was the first time the student council participated in a local cultural and creative event in Macau..

Sport

During my primary school years, I joined the school volleyball team and participated in inter-school competitions as well as various local tournaments. These experiences were very valuable to me. However, I decided to leave the volleyball team in my sophomore year of junior high. Nevertheless, I still have a strong passion for playing volleyball to this day.

Performance experience

I started learning classical music from a very young age. Music has boosted my self-confidence and provided me with many performance opportunities.

Exchange activities between CDSJ5 and Ricci Student Union

On February 19, 2023, we conducted an exchange activity with the student union of Colegio Mateus Ricci. Through various games and a barbecue, we deepened the communication between the members of the two student unions. Everyone gained a lot from the experience and made new friends in the process.


【Composition, Performance, and Production】Daze In White x 20Hz Summer Intensive Camp

I have always been interested in learning popular music, and I am very interested in both composition and arrangement. During the summer of 2023, I participated in the music intensive camp organized by the local band Daze In White, where I learned to create my own unique works and enhance my musical skills.

2024 HIGHWAY TO U.S. COLLEGES ENGLISH BOOTCAMP

I have always been interested in pursuing further studies in Hong Kong, so on March 21, 2024, I went to Hong Kong to attend a one-day exchange activity called the "HIGHWAY TO U.S. COLLEGES ENGLISH BOOTCAMP". During the event, I not only learned how to write a good personal statement, but also obtained a certificate and had the opportunity to exchange with students from different schools in Hong Kong.
